ERP Newsletter #55

29 November 2024

 

Updates from the programme

  

Gemma Moss, Charlotte Vidal-Hall, Alex Jones, Craig Skerritt, and Verena Braehler have been awarded the Best Educational research and educational policy making SIG presentation at BERA 2024 for a presentation called "The intersection between research and policy: discussing the perspectives, experiences and understandings of academic researchers and Ofsted".

 

As a member of the ESRC Research Leadership Network, Gemma will be attending a horizon-scanning event in the New Year, with Stian Westlake, ESRC Executive Chair.

 

Updates from the projects

 

Decentring the 'resilient teacher': exploring interactions between individuals and their social ecologies PI: Steph Ainsworth

 

The team have been providing wellbeing training in some of our case study schools and last week, a few of the primary teachers attended MMU for an externally organised event about pupil wellbeing by Teachappy. 

 

They have had papers and a symposium accepted for two conferences in the USA, ResilienceCon in Tennessee and CIES in Chicago.
