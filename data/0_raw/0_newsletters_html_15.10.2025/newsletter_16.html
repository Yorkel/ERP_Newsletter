The Foundation for Education Development (FED) is running an online event  on new technologies in education

‘Some have newness thrust upon them’ – managing innovation in education.

Thursday 30th November 9.30-10.45 Online

Book your place here.

Speakers:
David Jaffa - CEO Jaffa Foundation 
Dr Daniella Jenkins - Senior Lecturer Design Thinking and Innovation Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship University of Bristol
Elliot Seller - Head of Connected Home, Innovation and Partnerships Vodafone 

There is no hiding from new ideas or new technologies, but often the temptation is to pull up the school drawbridge or wait for Government to tell us what to do. But what can we learn from how new products are developed and tested and how can we build an education  system that helps best manage innovation? This is the first of three FEDSpace style sessions being run with Innovate-ED, a FED project that asks big questions about managing change. You can find out more about Innovate-ED and respond to its first thinkpiece here.
