EdTech

 

BERJ - Exploring the landscape of GenAI and education literature: A taxonomy of themes and sub‐themes

Sam Clarke

The research landscape surrounding Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI) and education is rapidly expanding, characterised by a dynamic array of themes and sub-themes. This paper aims to construct a comprehensive taxonomy that categorises the current literature on the integration of GenAI in educational settings.
