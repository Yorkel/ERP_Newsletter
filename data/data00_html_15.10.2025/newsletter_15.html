What Matters in Education?

 

 Children’s Commissioner - Relationship between school attendance and academic attainment 

Just over a third of pupils hit the threshold for either persistent or severe absenteeism in either Y10 or 11. Pupils who were persistently or severely absent in both years received worse GCSE grades than their peers.  Makes many recommendations, incl that: every school should appoint a member of the Senior Leadership Team to manage their attendance policy, DfE should roll out national training on school attendance and extend reach of Attendance Hubs.
