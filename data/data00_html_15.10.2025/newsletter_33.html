ERP Project: Investigating the recruitment and retention of ethnic minority teachers PI: Stephen Gorard

 

Ethnic disproportionality in the schools of England

On 24 April, the Durham team reported on emerging findings from their study of ethnic minority representation in the teaching work force and the extent to which it reflects the ethnic minority background of the student population. This was part of the Durham University School of Education Research Seminar series.

The emerging findings are based on three structured reviews of evidence, secondary data analyses of the School Workforce Census linked to the National Pupil Database, a national survey of teachers, and follow up interviews and case studies.
