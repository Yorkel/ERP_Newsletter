IPPO - Evaluation of IPPO’S Impact

As IPPO closes, they picked out some of the key learnings:

  1. There is a growing demand for evidence to inform public policy across the UK.
  2. Synthesising useful evidence for policymakers is complex and context-dependent.
  3. Exploring policy challenges across different jurisdictions and scales is beneficial.
  4. Dialogue is crucial for scoping policy demand, defining evidence questions, and addressing real-world challenges.
  5. The key lesson is that evidence is best absorbed by policymakers through conversation and interaction, not just through repositories and reports.
