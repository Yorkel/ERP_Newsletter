Rethinking teacher recruitment: New approaches to attracting prospective STEM teachers PI: Rob Klassen

 

The team have a new paper out - Measuring the effects of motivation messages on STEM undergraduates’ interest in teaching. Teaching and Teacher Education. It's by Sophie Thompson-Lee, Robert Klassen, Hui Wang and Rebecca Snell  Read it
